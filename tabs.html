Shipping

We offer complimentary standard shipping on all orders. Expedited shipping options are also available at checkout for an additional fee. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days.

Returns

We accept returns within 14 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return, items must be in their original, unworn condition. Please note that custom-made pieces are final sale and cannot be returned.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team. They will provide you with a prepaid shipping label and further instructions.

What materials do you use?

Our pieces are crafted from the finest materials, including 14k and 18k gold, platinum, and ethically sourced gemstones and diamonds.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes, we specialize in creating bespoke jewelry. Contact our design team to begin the process of bringing your vision to life.

How should I care for my jewelry?

We recommend cleaning your jewelry regularly with a soft cloth and mild soap. For a deeper clean, you can use a soft-bristled toothbrush and warm water.
